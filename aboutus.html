OUT STORY

Our Founder launched Wenwen with one goal: to elevate the nail salon experience.

We've thrown away the rulebook and reinvented it for the modern age, using years of industry research to perfect every aspect from start to finish. Say hello to flawless treatments, beautiful salons, and impeccable service that you can rely on every time.

THE WENWEN WAY

At Townhouse, we strive to deliver a Flawless Finish every time and are proud to offer the industry's first gel manicure guarantee.

It's simple - purchase any of our gel manicures and if you experience any issues, for any reason over the following 7 days, we will happily fix it for free. That's gel manis - stress free!

HEALTH & HYGIENE

The health of our customers and hygiene is our utmost priority. That's why all our tools are either single-use (and recycled) or sterilized in a hospital-grade autoclave machine between each use.

We also believe in 'better-for-you' products - all our polishes and gels are at least 3-free so you don't need to worry about toxic chemicals.
